Vadvanalstotra

Hanuman Vadvanal Stotra was written by Ravana’s brother Vibhishana. In this stotram,
Vibhishana praises the power of Hanuman and also prays for protection from all
diseases, diseases, enemies, fears, troubles. By chanting Hanuman Badabanala Stotram
for forty days with devotion and meditation, one can get rid of all kinds
of problems, especially the one’s related to health. All kinds of fever,
ghosts and enemies will be eliminated. Impossible can be achieved by chanting this
hymn. Get Hanuman Vadvanal Stotra in English lyrics here and chant it to attain immense benefits.
